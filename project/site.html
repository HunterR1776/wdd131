What we are about?

Precision Rifle Series (PRS) shooting is a competitive long-range shooting sport that challenges participants to engage targets at varying distances from multiple positions under time limits. Unlike traditional static bench shooting, PRS stages often require shooters to adapt to different props, terrain simulations, and target layouts, emphasizing problem-solving, consistency, and efficient movement. Success in PRS depends on mastering fundamentals such as stable positioning, trigger control, wind reading, and equipment management. The sport is known for its structured matches, supportive community, and strong emphasis on safety, discipline, and sportsmanship, making it both technically demanding and highly rewarding for dedicated competitors.
