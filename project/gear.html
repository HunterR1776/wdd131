What type of Gear do I need to Start

PRS-style shooting requires specialized gear designed to promote consistency, stability, and precision at extended distances. Competitors typically use a precision-built rifle—most commonly a bolt-action platform—paired with a high-quality optic that allows for clear target identification and accurate adjustments. Support equipment such as bipods, shooting bags, and tripods helps shooters build stable positions on various props and surfaces encountered during stages. Reliable ammunition, eye and ear protection, and proper data tracking tools (such as a data book or ballistic app) are also essential components of a well-prepared setup. While equipment can enhance performance, success ultimately depends on safe handling, solid fundamentals, and the shooter’s ability to apply their skills under match conditions.
