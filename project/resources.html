Saftey and Education

Education and safety are foundational to PRS-style precision rifle shooting. Before focusing on performance or competition, shooters are expected to understand and consistently apply core firearm safety principles, including safe muzzle direction, proper handling, and awareness of their surroundings. Formal training—whether through certified instructors, local clubs, or structured competition environments—helps participants develop safe gun handling habits, range etiquette, and responsible equipment management. PRS matches are run with strict safety briefings, clearly defined firing lines, and range officer oversight to ensure that every stage is conducted in a controlled and disciplined manner. Emphasizing education not only improves consistency and confidence, but also reinforces the culture of responsibility and sportsmanship that defines the precision shooting community
